Help

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Join with us" button and fill in the required information such as your name, email, and password.

How do I login?

To login, click on the "Login" button and enter your email and password.

How do I create a post?

To create a post, click on the "New Post" button and fill in the required information such as the post title, content, and any relevant tags.

How do I search for posts?

To search for posts, use the search bar and enter keywords or relevant tags. You can also use the filters to refine your search results.

How do I comment to posts?

To comment on a post, go to the post and scroll down to the "Comments" section. Type your comment in the text box and click on the "Post Comment" button. Your comment will be posted below the existing comments.
